Welcome to MediFam

We are now open! MediFam is an independent telehealth group founded by board-certified physicians. We distribute health-related services and information through telecommunication technologies.This method gives us the opportunity to follow up with our patients from wherever they are, give care, advice, reminders, education, monitoring and remote admissions.

We are happy to provide care for all ages including underserved communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide patients with the best, most comprehensive care from the young to older adults. Our practice is to commited to offering our patients a more convenient way to connect with doctors. We seek to provide care to underserved populations in areas where there are limited community resources and high levels of illiteracy and poverty. Quality of care and experience is at the core of everything we do and every decision we make.

Our Vision

We believe that everyone regardless of age or status should have the potential to become healthy and productive members of their community. Our doctors provide general heath care and coordinate with specialists as needed. We want to foster a relationship-based care built on respect of patients' unique needs, cultures, values, and preferences.

Our Values

MediFam was established by physicians of the National Health Forum who believe in our core values that form the company's culture:

  • Quality:

    We maintain the highest standards and achieve them by continually measuring and improving our outcomes.

  • Service:

    We strive to exceed our patients and/or fellow caregivers expectations for comfort and convenience.

  • Innovation:

    We welcome change, encourage invention and continually seek better, more efficient ways to achieve our goals.

  • Team work:

    We collaborate and share knowledge to benefit patients and fellow caregivers for the advancement of our mission.

  • Integrity:

    We adhere to high moral principles and professional standards by a commitment to honesty, confidentiality, trust, respect and transparency.
